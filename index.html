TRIBUTE TO DOCTORS




The list of the sleep-deprived heroes during tough situation of pandemic includes doctors, nurses, medical cleaners, pathologists, paramedics, ambulance drivers, and health-care administrators.

The fight against coronavirus, the brave medical army stands strong with thermometers, stethoscopes, and ventilators as their weapons. Not to forget, medical researchers are working day in and night out against all odds, hoping to find the antidote to the disease.

Dear Doctor, thank you for everything you have done for us to keep us in the best of our health. Your efforts have made us a healthier soul.

Not all heroes wear capes. In the midst of the novel coronavirus pandemic, the real heroes wear scrubs.
